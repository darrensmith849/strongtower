# Strong Tower — Response Templates

**Audience:** the team replying to pilot, support, and contact submissions.

**How to use:**
- Pick the template that matches the submitter's role/category.
- Personalise the opener and the question. Always use their name.
- Edit anything that doesn't fit the person. Templates are starting points, not scripts.
- Keep tone: warm, gospel-centred, non-shaming, clear, honest, invitation-based — never salesy.
- Never claim device protection is live. Never use commercial language ("upgrade", "premium", "subscribe", "customers", etc.).
- Never ask for or include graphic detail.

Every template ends with a clear next step and a quiet door — *no pressure to reply*.

## What never to do

- Never claim protection is live.
- Never claim a specific launch date you can't keep.
- Never ask for or include graphic detail of what someone has viewed or done.
- Never use the words *pricing*, *plans*, *upgrade*, *premium*, *subscribe*, *buy now*, *customers*, *paid tier*.
- Never use the words *tamper-proof*, *impossible to bypass*, *guaranteed freedom*, *guaranteed protection*.
- Never present Strong Tower as clinical, medical, or crisis care.
- Never share another person's submission, even in private chat, without explicit permission.
- Never reply in anger or with a punitive tone, even when an inbound message is rude.
